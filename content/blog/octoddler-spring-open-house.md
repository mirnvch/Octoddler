## You're Invited to Discover Octoddler!

Spring is the perfect time for new beginnings, and we invite your family to join us for **Octoddler's Spring Open House** on **March 15-16, 2026**. Whether you're just beginning to explore Montessori education or ready to find your child's school home, this is your opportunity to experience Octoddler firsthand.

## What to Expect

### Guided Classroom Tours

Step into our beautiful Montessori environments and see what makes Octoddler special. You'll have the opportunity to:

- **Explore our prepared environments** with child-sized furniture, Montessori materials, and thoughtfully arranged learning areas
- **Observe children at work** (in classrooms where sessions are in progress) to see the Montessori method in action
- **Ask questions** about our daily routines, curriculum, and approach to early childhood education

### Meet Our Experienced Teachers

Our passionate, certified Montessori teachers are the heart of Octoddler. During the open house, you'll have time to:

- **Meet the educators** who would guide your child's learning journey
- **Learn about their Montessori training** and teaching philosophy
- **Discuss your child's individual needs** and how we support each child's development

### Q&A Sessions

We understand that choosing a preschool comes with many questions. Our directors and teachers will be available to address topics such as:

- Daily schedules and routines
- Enrollment process and availability
- Montessori philosophy and methods
- Transition and separation support
- Parent communication and involvement
- Tuition and program options

### Refreshments and Community

Connect with other families considering Octoddler while enjoying light refreshments. Many current Octoddler parents will be present to share their experiences and answer questions from a parent perspective.

## Open House Locations and Times

Our open house takes place at all four Octoddler locations:

**Saturday, March 15, 2026**
- Downtown Campus: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Westside Campus: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

**Sunday, March 16, 2026**
- Northpark Campus: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Riverside Campus: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Choose the location and time that works best for your family. You're welcome to visit multiple campuses if you'd like to explore different environments.

## How to RSVP

Space is limited to ensure a quality experience for every family, so please reserve your spot in advance.

**To RSVP**: Visit our [Schedule a Tour](/contact) page and select "Spring Open House" as your preferred visit type. Choose your preferred campus and time slot. We'll send you a confirmation email with parking information and what to expect.

**Can't make these dates?** No problem! We offer individual tours year-round. Simply visit our contact page to schedule a private tour at your convenience.

## Come As You Are

There's no need to prepare anything special—just bring your curiosity and questions! Children are welcome to attend with you, though having childcare arranged allows for deeper conversation with our team.

Dress comfortably, as you'll have the option to sit on floor mats and explore materials alongside your child.

## Why Visit Octoddler?

Choosing your child's first school is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a parent. An open house visit allows you to:

- Experience the Montessori difference firsthand
- Assess whether our community feels like the right fit for your family
- Envision your child thriving in our nurturing environments
- Get answers to all your questions in person

## We Can't Wait to Meet You

At Octoddler, we believe that education begins with respect, wonder, and community. Our open house is designed to help you discover whether our Montessori approach aligns with your family's values and your child's needs.

Whether your child is an infant, toddler, or preschooler, we have programs designed to support their unique developmental journey. Come see what makes Octoddler a special place where children develop independence, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

**Mark your calendars for March 15-16, 2026**, and [reserve your spot today](/contact). We look forward to welcoming your family to the Octoddler community!

Have questions before the open house? Email us at hello@octoddler.school or call any of our campus locations. We're here to help!
