## Welcome to Spring 2025 Enrollment

We're excited to announce that enrollment is now open for our Spring 2025 session! Whether you're new to Montessori education or continuing your child's journey with us, we have wonderful opportunities available.

### What's New This Spring

Our spring session brings several exciting additions:

- **Extended hours** at our Sunnyvale location
- **New outdoor learning spaces** at all campuses
- **Enhanced art and music programs**
- **Parent education workshops** on Montessori principles

## Available Programs

### Toddler Community (18 months - 3 years)

Our toddler program focuses on:

- Independence in daily activities
- Language development
- Sensorial exploration
- Practical life skills

**Spaces available at:** Sunnyvale, Mountain View

### Primary Program (3 - 6 years)

The primary classroom offers:

1. Academic preparation in math, language, and science
2. Cultural studies and geography
3. Creative expression through art and music
4. Social and emotional development

**Spaces available at:** Sunnyvale, Mountain View, Palo Alto

## Enrollment Process

### Step 1: Schedule a Tour

Visit one of our campuses to see Montessori in action. Tours are available:

- Weekday mornings at 9:30 AM
- Select afternoons by appointment
- Virtual tours for your convenience

### Step 2: Submit Application

Complete our online application form. *Early applications receive priority consideration.*

### Step 3: Parent Interview

Meet with our director to discuss your child's needs and our program philosophy.

### Step 4: Enrollment Confirmation

Once accepted, secure your spot with enrollment deposit.

## Important Dates

**February 15:** Early enrollment deadline (receive $100 discount)

**March 1:** Regular enrollment deadline

**April 7:** Spring session begins
